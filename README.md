maddymeta helps you manage website metadata. You use this tool to inspect how your site looks on search engines and social media platforms. It offers a scoring system to help you refine your content for better visibility.

## 🛠️ What this tool does

Metadata tells search engines and social platforms what your webpage contains. When you share a link on Facebook, X, or Telegram, the image and text you see come from your metadata. If these tags are missing or incorrect, your link looks unprofessional. 

maddymeta solves these problems through several features:

* **Live Previews:** See exactly how your site appears on Google, Facebook, and Twitter.
* **SEO Scoring:** Get a score based on your meta tag quality.
* **Fix Assistant:** Receive advice to correct common metadata errors.
* **Fast Analysis:** Scan your site in seconds to identify missing information.

## 📈 Understanding your SEO score

The scoring engine evaluates your site based on standard requirements for search engines and social networks. A high score suggests your tags contain all necessary information for platforms to display your link correctly. A low score indicates missing pieces like an image or a description.

* **Title Tags:** This shows the main text in search results. Ensure this describes your page content clearly.
* **Description Tags:** This text appears under your title in search results. Users read this to decide whether to click your link.
* **Open Graph Tags:** These control how your site appears on Facebook and other platforms.
* **Twitter Cards:** These dictate the image and size of your post on X.

## 🛡️ Privacy and data protection

maddymeta processes your web pages locally on your computer. The application does not store your website content on external servers during the analysis process. Your data remains on your machine while the application generates previews. Only the necessary requests are sent to social platforms to fetch the live preview images you see in the software interface.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting common problems

If you experience issues during installation or usage, check these common items:

* **File blocked:** Windows might show a safety warning. Select "More info" and then "Run anyway" if the system prevents the installer from opening.
* **No preview images:** Ensure your internet connection functions properly. Preview images depend on external servers hosted by Facebook or X.
* **Application does not start:** Verify that you installed the software using the correct Windows version. Try restarting your computer before opening the application again.
* **Analysis fails:** Check that you typed the full website address starting with `https://` or `http://`.
